

Disobeying the School Rules

George hugged his school bag tightly as he walked into school. Looking around furtively, he went up to his classroom to leave his bag on his seat. He had his new game console in his bag. It was against the school rules to take it to school but he could not resist the thought of playing with it during recess.

After the morning assembly in the courtyard, George went back to class with the rest of his classmates. He could hardly concentrate on his work as his thoughts were on the game console in his bag. He was waiting for his Mathematics teacher to come for class when a prefect came into the classroom to tell everyone that their Mathematics teacher was absent from school that day. The class was told to read a book quietly. A sudden quiver of excitement shot through George as he thought of the game console in his bag. He was sitting in a corner at the back of the classroom. If he took out a storybook and made it stand open on his table, he would be able to place his game console in front of it. All he needed to do was to turn off the volume so that no one would know what he was doing.

George was so absorbed in his game that he did not realize that the entire class was sitting in rapt attention. The principal had walked into the classroom and was giving them work to do. She did not notice George at first but when she did, she called out to him. Her voice rang out in the silent classroom but George was so engrossed in his game that he did not hear her. She strode up to him and towered over him. Noticing the shadow falling across his game, George looked up in irritation. His eyes widened in horror when he saw that the person was none other than his principal. She looked at him grimly. Her mouth was set in a straight line and her eyes narrowed as she stared at him. George expected her to rail at him but instead, she silently reached over and grabbed his game console.

"I want to see your parents before I give this back to you," she said sternly.

George's heart sank. He was almost in tears as he sat through the rest of the lesson. He loved his new toy so much that he definitely wanted it back. He would have to tell his parents what had happened. He was certain that they would punish him. If only he had left the game console at home.
